4/19

Mr. Wiffleball

Join us for an exciting night as 12 lovely gentlemen compete for the coveted title of Mr. Wiffleball in their group dance, swimsuit introductions, minigame challenge, talent performances, and formal wear/Q&A! This year's show addresses toxic masculinity in the Asian American and Pacific Islander (AAPI) community and attempts to redefine AAPI masculinity in a Crazy Rich Asians theme.
